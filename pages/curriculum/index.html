Curriculum

When designing a custom curriculum at Starkeep Academy, mapping out future constellation paths is essential for maintaining a clear vision of your goals. Constellation paths are clusters of constellations whose achievements build on one another to culminate in a journey of lifelong growth and mastery.

Upon enrolling, a student must choose two foundational templates before registering for courses: one Heroic Path and one Learning Path. To determine the best fit, students undertake a preliminary trial—an immersive series of games and quizzes that evaluate their unique aptitudes. While the Academy will suggest suitable paths based on these results, the final choice always belongs to the student, and paths can be changed if a student discovers a new calling. Choosing the right combination of these paths is the ultimate way to tailor your Starkeep experience:

The Heroic Path

(The "Who, What, and Why"): This path dictates the subjects, skills, and way of life the student will pursue. Operating as an archetype or vocation, it defines who the student wants to become and the impact they wish to make. Students who share a Heroic Path are grouped into Guilds, and this path serves as the thematic template for all future milestones. These paths direct the student towards their goal through the emulation of heroic archetypes. They dictate the way a student applies their knowledge and skill to the real world.

The Learning Path

(The "How"): If the Heroic Path dictates the content of your education, the Learning Path dictates its structure. It determines how your milestones branch out, where you focus your attention, and how you best absorb information. This template customizes the style and pace of your journey. These constellation paths help students structure their education to suit their inherent nature and scope of interest. They dictate the methods and goals by which a student acquires their knowledge and skill.
