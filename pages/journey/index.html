Intro

Journey

A student's personal and academic growth is tracked and intuitively displayed through our platform's Star Map interface. When selected, this immersive quest journal transports the user to a starry observatory where their achievements float and glow in the space above them.

Step 01 · The Waypoint

Milestones

In the Academy, assignments, projects, tests, and homework start as Milestones. Generated when you enroll in a course, accept a Guild mission, or create a plan with your mentor, they act as future waypoints on the Heroic Journey toward your North Star.

They help you prioritise tasks and track pending activities — the promises you make to your future self, not yet earned but already glowing on the horizon.

Milestones are generated automatically and serve as the starting point of every achievement in your Star Map.
Step 02 · The Achievement

Stars

Every completed Milestone transforms into an achievement badge called a Star. Each star is the glowing symbol of a completed challenge in the Starkeeper's path.

Stars allow you to view and catalog any related documentation on that achievement — regardless of format. Your record of growth, permanently held in your sky.

Stars are yours forever. Tap any one to open its full record — proof of who you were becoming when you earned it.
Step 03 · The Legend

Constellations

A specific curriculum module is made up of a cluster of Milestones. When a student completes all the Stars within that cluster, it becomes a Constellation.

This constellation arranges itself into a distinct symbol in your sky — like a wolf or a sword — becoming a visual myth of your unfolding personal legend.

Every constellation is a chapter of your story, permanently written into the sky above you for all to witness.
