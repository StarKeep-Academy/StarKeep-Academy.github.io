Who We Are

Follow your
North Star

A "North Star" is a distant but potential reality —
a guiding light toward a future we hope to achieve.

The future it represents is not a fantasy, but a destination we can reach if we only follow in its direction. A North Star is a compass towards this ideal world, and our ideal selves.

We are Atelier Infinity, a design studio and social enterprise whose purpose is to revive the pursuit of the North Star by inspiring, empowering, and educating visionary youth who will one day change the world.

We are a collective of dreamers, designers, and innovators- Starkeepers who feel the calling to follow our own North Star. A Starkeeper is someone who envisions an ideal for society and themselves and actively works towards embodying those ideals.

It could look like a world where all neglected animals end up in a safe haven, or where all pollution has been eradicated from the world’s oceans. This star of hope influences our daily actions to bring the world ever closer to our vision of paradise. Right now, an ideal world may not be within our grasp, yet we all have the power to improve the world we live in- one decision, one action, one person at a time.

This is the Starkeeper’s way of life–to set our sights on the brightest star in the sky and cultivate that celestial flame in every day spent on this Earth.

What We Do

Starkeepers

A collective of dreamers, designers, and innovators who feel the calling to follow their own North Star.

Modern society has lost sight of its North Star, leaving many to wonder how we can change the world when we feel no motivation, have no direction, and lack the resources to change our own lives. To remedy this lack of direction, we at Atelier Infinity are developing a new type of learning platform: Starkeep Academy. Accessible via smartphone, internet browser, or immersive virtual reality, Starkeep Academy is an online school whose curriculum is, in essence, a global alternate reality game, blending fantasy and reality to co-create a better future. In our school, you enroll to learn how to change the world. Upon enrollment, you are guided to set your sights on an impossible dream—one you've had in the depths of your heart since childhood: your North Star. You then create a heroic role-play persona that can achieve that dream. Finally, we design your own custom curriculum where you can make that dream your reality. The Academy’s fundamental mission is to mentor driven youth by providing them with a revolutionary learning program that gamifies the process of world change. As you learn, your visions are refined, developed & installed around the Academy, expanding the virtual campus into a utopian eco-city that showcases the innovations of tomorrow. At Starkeep Academy, we strive to not just role-play as heroes, but become real ones. Your progress is dictated by your real heroic actions, whether that is planting a tree or designing a sustainable invention. These academic achievements become spatialized data visualizations, allowing you to see your progress grow in real-time—not as a static degree or certification, but as a starry sky filled with your own personal growth. Through this platform, heroic goals are framed in the context of social entrepreneurship. Each world-changing idea is scaled into a startup initiative that the student learns how to successfully launch and fundraise with the help of industry experts, course facilitators, and university lecturers. Our students' progress is broadcast to the world, culminating in a yearly competitive exam that generates buzz and sponsorship around these young role models’ journeys to achieving their ultimate goals.
