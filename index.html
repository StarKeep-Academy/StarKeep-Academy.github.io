Who We Are

Follow your
North Star

We are Atelier Infinity, a design studio and social enterprise whose purpose is to revive the pursuit of the North Star by inspiring, empowering, and educating visionary youth who will one day change the world.

A "North Star" is a distant but potential reality —
a guiding light toward a future we hope to achieve.

The future it represents is not a fantasy, but a destination we can reach if we only follow in its direction. A North Star is a compass towards this ideal world, and our ideal selves.

We are a collective of dreamers, designers, and innovators—Starkeepers who feel the calling to follow our own North Star.

What We Do

Starkeepers

Upon enrollment, you are guided to set your sights on an impossible dream—one you've had in the depths of your heart since childhood: your North Star.

You then create a heroic role-play persona that can achieve that dream. Finally, we design your own custom curriculum where you can make that dream your reality.

Our Purpose

There are 4 ideals
to Starkeep Academy's ethos:

Solarpunk Solutions

A Starkeeper always aims to build Solarpunk solutions that balance nature, innovation, and social welfare to create an optimistic and regenerative outcome. By focusing on accessible technology and grassroots empowerment, we aim to design a future that benefits all life.


Heroic Method

A Starkeeper always aims to embody their highest potential through the practice of Avatarism, a form of roleplay activism that sets the heroic alter ego as a guideline for daily growth. We transform the fantasy of being a superhero into a practical, actionable framework for real-world self-improvement.


Narrative Medium

A Starkeeper always aims to integrate Storytelling into their education, expression, and presentation, as narrative is central to the lifespan, virality, and transmission of human knowledge. By treating personal and entrepreneurial growth as an unfolding origin story, we ensure these journeys inspire the wider world.


Serious Play

A Starkeeper always aims to make all of their actions a form of Play, where fun and flow guide all steps toward a brighter future. We remove the modern stigma of "childishness" from play, using engaging challenges to drive our motivation for tackling serious global issues.

Our Vision

The Academy's
Fundamental Mission

To mentor driven youth by providing them with a revolutionary learning program that gamifies the process of world change. As you learn, your visions are refined, developed & installed around the Academy, expanding the virtual campus into a utopian eco-city that showcases the innovations of tomorrow.

At Starkeep Academy, we strive to not just role-play as heroes, but become real ones. Your progress is dictated by your real heroic actions, whether that is planting a tree or designing a sustainable invention. These academic achievements become spatialized data visualizations, allowing you to see your progress grow in real-time—not as a static degree or certification, but as a starry sky filled with your own personal growth.
